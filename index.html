Animal website

Welcome to animal website. Here you will get everything you want to know about animals. Animals are divided into three groups regarding which way they give birth to their young ones,their body texture and style,etc.. These three groups are:

  • Mammals
  • Reptiles
  • Amphibians

Mammals

Mammals are the animals which give birth to their young ones. For example: humans,some fishes like whale,cow,dinkey,horse,etc.. Most mammals live in land.

Reptiles

Reptiles are the animals which lay eggs to give birth to their young ones. for example:chickens,most of the fishes,crocodiles,snakes,etc..

Amphibians

Amphibians also lay eggs to give birth to their young ones. They live both in land and in water. For example:frogs,salamenders,etc..
